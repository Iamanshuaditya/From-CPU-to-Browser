# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to a respectful, constructive, and inclusive learning and collaboration environment.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and specific in feedback.
- Focus on ideas, evidence, and implementation details.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Share reproducible examples when reporting issues.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior.
- Repeated low-signal disruption after guidance.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation for conduct violations.

## Reporting
Report conduct issues through repository maintainers with context and evidence.
